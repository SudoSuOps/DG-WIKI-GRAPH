## What an OM Tells You (Unpacked)

A typical Dollar General OM from a broker contains these sections. Agent-DG generates these automatically from the DG wiki data:

```
SECTION 1: INVESTMENT SUMMARY
  Offering price, cap rate, NOI, price/SF
  Lease type (NNN vs NN), term remaining
  Corporate guarantee (BBB/Baa2)
  Rent bump schedule
  → Agent-DG source: cap-rate-benchmarks.md + lease-structures.md

SECTION 2: TENANT OVERVIEW
  Dollar General Corp profile
  20,959 stores, 48 states, $41.2B revenue
  Credit rating history, stock performance
  → Agent-DG source: dollar-general-corporate.md

SECTION 3: PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
  Address, building size, lot size, year built
  Aerial photo, property photo, site plan
  Parking count, ingress/egress, visibility
  → Agent-DG source: store data + county assessor

SECTION 4: LEASE ABSTRACT
  Commencement date, expiration date
  Base rent, bump schedule with dates
  Option periods with renewal rent
  NNN responsibility matrix
  → Agent-DG source: dg-lease-template.md

SECTION 5: MARKET & LOCATION
  Demographics (1/3/5 mile radius)
  Traffic counts, household income
  Population growth, employment
  Competitive retail landscape
  → Agent-DG source: site-selection.md + fl-market-overview.md

SECTION 6: COMPARABLE SALES
  5 most recent DG trades in area
  Cap rate, price, NOI, lease term for each
  Market cap rate trend line
  → Agent-DG source: this file (recent-trades.md) + cap-rate-benchmarks.md

SECTION 7: FINANCIAL ANALYSIS
  Cash flow projection (year 1 through lease expiry)
  Rent bump impact on NOI over time
  Reversion value at exit (various cap rate scenarios)
  IRR analysis (5yr, 7yr, 10yr hold periods)
  → Agent-DG source: calculated from lease terms + market caps
```

## Agent-DG Comp Deed Format

Every trade gets filed as a comp deed in the DG wiki — same 1-sheet format:

```
COMP DEED (1 sheet, folded in half):
  
  COVER:
    [PHOTO]
    SOLD — [ADDRESS]
    [CITY], [STATE] [ZIP]
    PRICE: $[PRICE] | CAP: [CAP_RATE]% | DATE: [CLOSE_DATE]
    
  INSIDE LEFT:
    Parcel: [PARCEL_ID] | County: [COUNTY]
    Building: [SQFT] SF on [LOT_SIZE] acres
    Year Built: [YEAR] | Parking: [SPACES]
    Lease: [TYPE] — [TERM_REMAINING] years remaining
    
  INSIDE RIGHT:
    NOI: $[NOI] | Rent/SF: $[RENT_PSF]
    Bumps: [BUMP_SCHEDULE]
    Buyer: [BUYER_ENTITY] ([BUYER_TYPE])
    Seller: [SELLER_ENTITY] (held [HOLD_YEARS] years)
    Price appreciation: [APPRECIATION]% from [PURCHASE_PRICE]
    
  BACK:
    Verified by: Swarm & Bee Tribunal
    Confidence: [SCORE] | Source: [SOURCE]
    Filed: [DEED_DATE]
```

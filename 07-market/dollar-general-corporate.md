## Real Estate Structure — Critical for STNL

DG operates primarily as a TENANT, not a building owner.

```
OWNERSHIP MODEL:
  ~80% of stores are LEASED from third-party landlords
  ~20% are company-owned (land and/or building)
  
  THIS MEANS: For ~16,700 stores, there is a LANDLORD
  That landlord is the target for Agent-DG
  DG Corp is the TENANT — investment-grade credit (BBB)
  
TYPICAL LEASE STRUCTURE:
  Primary term: 10-15 years
  Options: Multiple 5-year renewal options
  Rent bumps: Typically 10% every 5 years
  Type: Triple Net (NNN) — tenant pays taxes, insurance, CAM
  
  This is the GOLD STANDARD for net lease investors:
  - Investment-grade tenant (BBB)
  - Long-term lease (10-15 years)
  - Predictable bumps (10% every 5 years)
  - NNN = landlord has zero management responsibility
```

## DSCR Requirements

```
INVESTMENT-GRADE TENANT (DG = BBB):
  Minimum DSCR: 1.20x (most lenders)
  Preferred DSCR: 1.25x+ (best rates)
  
  EXAMPLE (typical DG deal):
    Purchase price:      $1,574,000 (market avg)
    Loan at 75% LTV:     $1,180,500
    Rate:                5.88% (CMBS)
    Amortization:        25 years
    Annual debt service:  $88,800
    NOI:                 $103,651
    DSCR:                $103,651 ÷ $88,800 = 1.167x
    
    At 1.167x — borderline. Lender may require:
      → Lower LTV (70% → DSCR improves to 1.25x)
      → OR higher rate (6.25% → but DSCR stays tight)
      → OR rate buydown via points
    
    NEW CONSTRUCTION DG ($120K NOI):
    DSCR: $120,000 ÷ $88,800 = 1.35x ← EASY approval
    
    This is why new construction DGs trade at tighter caps.
    Higher NOI = higher DSCR = easier financing = more buyers = lower cap rate.
```

## Lender Types by Deal Size

```
UNDER $1M (small DG, secondary market):
  Best source: local bank or credit union
  Why: relationship lending, know the market, fast close
  Challenge: may require recourse (personal guarantee)
  
$1M - $3M (typical DG deal):
  Best source: CMBS conduit or regional bank
  Why: non-recourse CMBS available at this size, competitive rates
  Players: Arbor, CBRE, Newmark, Berkadia, Walker & Dunlop
  
$3M - $10M (portfolio or new construction):
  Best source: life company or CMBS
  Why: lowest rates (life co), max leverage (CMBS)
  Players: MetLife, Prudential, NYL, Principal, Pacific Life
  
$10M+ (portfolio acquisition):
  Best source: CMBS or bank syndication
  Why: scale pricing, portfolio diversification credit
  Players: JP Morgan, Wells Fargo, Goldman Sachs (CMBS shelf)
```

## 1031 Exchange Financing

```
THE 1031 BUYER'S FINANCING CHALLENGE:
  Must close within 180 days of selling prior property
  Must identify replacement within 45 days
  Lender must underwrite and close FAST
  
  WHO FINANCES 1031 DG PURCHASES:
  
  1. ALL CASH (30-40% of 1031 DG deals):
     No financing needed. Close in 21 days.
     Buyer deploys full equity from prior sale.
     Tightest cap rates — cash buyers pay premium for speed.
  
  2. CMBS (30-35%):
     Rate lock available day 1. Non-recourse.
     60-75 day close timeline. Cuts it close on 180 days.
     Works if buyer sells prior property early in the 180 window.
  
  3. PORTFOLIO BANK (20-25%):
     Relationship lender. Fast underwriting.
     Can close in 30-45 days. Recourse usually required.
     Best for repeat 1031 buyers with banking relationship.
  
  4. DST (10-15%):
     No individual financing — DST sponsor finances at entity level.
     Buyer purchases beneficial interest, not real property.
     Fastest "close" — just wire funds to DST sponsor.
```
